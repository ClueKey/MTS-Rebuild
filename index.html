Who we are and what we do.

At McLain Technology Solutions (MTS), we believe that you don’t have to be a large corporation to have the best IT solutions for your business. We work with many partners to provide the best solutions and service to fit your needs. Whether you have a one-man shop or hundreds of employees, we can provide the support and solutions you need to keep your business running smoothly. MTS has years of experience supporting large corporate networks with multiple interconnected locations and high security requirements. We have over a decade of experience with the design, set up and maintenance of high security computer systems that are NISPOM compliant. We have seen firsthand what ransomware attacks can do to companies and we’re here to help protect yours. We have helped to retrieve data after ransomware attacks and helped put solutions in place to better protect systems and reduce the risk of those attacks. We understand that the Always-On Enterprise, virtual servers and cloud solutions are becoming increasingly more important to companies. We have the experience and solutions you need to keep your business running smoothly 24x7.
